2.4. Windows menu

“Show window” is a group to show a static window. Such windows as elements or diagram editors are not present in the group. To show many windows, it is more preferred to change active workspace.

“Workspace” is a group of available workspaces. You can also switch a workspace by pressing a button on a right top corner of the main window.

“Look And Feel” is a group of switch a look of the application.
